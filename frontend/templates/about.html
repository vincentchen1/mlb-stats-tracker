About Me

Hi! My name is Vincent Chen, a junior studying statistics at UC Berkeley.

I originally started out as a computer science major at Cal State Fullerton but I soon realized that I was more interested in working with data/business analytics and finding patterns in numbers.

Baseball has always been a big part of my life. I grew up playing and watching baseball, and over time I became fascinated by the stats behind the sport. That curiosity has inspired me to build this project as a way for people to track and explore baseball statistics on their own.

Over the past couple of years, I've also gotten into sports betting and daily fantasy on apps like PrizePicks and Underdog. By using simple data analysis, I've profited more than $2,500, which showed me how powerful data-driven decisions can be. With this app, I hope to make it easier for others to do their own research, learn about pitcher versus batter matchups, and discover the fun side of baseball analytics!
